Biography

I'm a Research Staff Member at IBM T. J. Watson Research Center, where I'm leading a research team (\textbf{technical lead}) on machine comprehension, question answering and related topics. I received my PhD degree from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) in June 2013. Before joining IBM, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in Brandeis University for one year, where my research topic is Chinese syntactic parsing.

Research Interests

Deep Learning, Question Answering, Machine Comprehension, Sentence Matching, Syntactic/Semantic Parsing, Machine Translation
